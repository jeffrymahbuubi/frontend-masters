Intro

My name is Aunuun Jeffry Mahbuubi. I am a resident of Malang, East Java, Indonesia.

Currently I work at NCKU, Taiwan as Research Assistant in a lab called Wearable Technology and Medical Health Applications. I worked on various project related to implementation of AI on medical topics such as: Medical Image Processing and Biomedical Signal Processing
